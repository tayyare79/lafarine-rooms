About the Hotel

A place that feels like Fethiye

Lafarine Rooms is a small, personal hotel in the centre of Fethiye — a town on Turkey's Turquoise Coast where the mountains meet the Aegean. 17 rooms, a handful of staff who know the city by heart, and a quiet confidence that comes from doing less, better.

We are two minutes from the bazaar, five minutes from the harbour, and ten minutes from wherever you need to be. The old town wraps around us — its light, its noise, its warmth — and that is precisely the point.

No conference rooms. No pools with sunbeds in rows. Just good rooms, genuine hospitality, and Fethiye outside your door.
